We follow a simplified version of the Contributor Covenant to keep the community welcoming and respectful.

## Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Using inclusive and respectful language
- Being empathetic to differing viewpoints
- Focusing on constructive feedback

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, insults, derogatory comments
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Scope
This applies in all project spaces and when representing the project in public.
